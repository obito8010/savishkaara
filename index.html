ABOUT SAVISHKAARA


Savishkaara is the annual techfest of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, organized by the Department of Computer Science and IT. It brings together innovators and technology enthusiasts through competitions, workshops, hackathons, and expert sessions, focusing on emerging technologies like AI, cybersecurity, robotics, and IoT.

With a perfect blend of learning and innovation, Savishkaara provides a platform to showcase talent, exchange ideas, and engage with industry experts. Join us to explore the future of technology and be part of an inspiring journey.
