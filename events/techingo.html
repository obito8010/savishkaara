About the Event

TechinGO Challenge is a two-round competition testing technical knowledge and quick thinking. Round 1 features a tech-based crossword, with the top three teams advancing. In Round 2, teams play a timed Bingo-style quiz, claiming boxes by answering correctly. The first team to complete a row, column, or diagonal wins, or the team with the most boxes if no Bingo is achieved.
